## Portfolio & Balances

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Check my balances">
    **Examples:**
    - "Show my Solana balances"
    - "What's my total portfolio value?"
    - "List all my wallets"
    - "How much ETH do I have?"
    - "Show my USDC balance on Base"

    **Tools used:** `get_balance`, `get_portfolio`, `list_wallets`

    **Learn more:** [Chains and Funding](/docs/guides/chains-and-funding)

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Track portfolio performance">
    **Examples:**
    - "How has my portfolio performed this week?"
    - "Show my transaction volume breakdown"
    - "What's my 30-day P&L?"
    - "Show me my trading history"

    **Tools used:** `get_portfolio` (with `include_history`), `get_portfolio_analytics`, `get_transaction_history`

    **Learn more:** [Chains and Funding](/docs/guides/chains-and-funding)

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Get deposit addresses">
    **Examples:**
    - "Give me my Solana deposit address"
    - "Show a QR code for receiving USDC on Base"
    - "What's my ETH address?"
    - "Where can I send funds to this wallet?"

    **Tools used:** `get_deposit_info`, `get_wallet_info`

    **Learn more:** [Chains and Funding](/docs/guides/chains-and-funding)

  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
